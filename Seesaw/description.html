Moment generated by two forces

The following app shows the effect of two forces acting on the two ends of a $40\,\mathrm{m}$ long beam. Both forces are oriented in the same direction and the beam is simply supported between the forces at point $A$. The length between the left end of the structure and the support position $a$ can be changed using the slider on the right.

In order to maintain equilibrium, the moments generated by the forces must have the same absolute value. The equilibrium is defined as \[ \sum M_A:\quad F_1a = F_2 (l-a). \] According to the equilibrium a change of the position of the support must be compensated by a change of the force amplitudes.

The values of $F_1$ and $F_2$ can be displayed using the "Show values" button.
