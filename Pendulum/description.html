Pendulum

This app shows the motion of a pendulum initially inclined of an angle φ₀ with respect to the vertical position and subjected to an initial angular velocity φ₀˙.

The energy balance shows the amount of kinetic energy during the oscillation, which reaches its minimum at the reversal point of the oscillation direction. The phase diagram shows the angular velocity φ˙ against the oscillation angle φ. This diagram shows clearly the damped behaviour for different values of the damping.

The interactivity parameters are

  • the initial angle φ₀
  • the initial velocity φ₀˙
  • the mass in kg
  • the damping constant in Ns/m
It is possible to switch to a double pendulum, also on the fly. The reset button brings the sysetm back to the initiaol settings.
