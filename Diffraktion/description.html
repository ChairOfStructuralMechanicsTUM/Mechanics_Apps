Diffraction of sound waves at barriers

In the following app we visualize the diffraction of planar waves at the end of a barrier. This phenomenom is important in the context of noise protection walls at e.g. motorways: noise is even transported into the shadow area of the wall by diffraction.

We allow the modification of wavelength γ and angle of incident φ0 of the wave. By clicking inside the countour plot, the noise level at this spot in dB can be probed and, therefore, the noise level at different positions and the effectivity of the barrier can be quantified.
