Mohr's Circle

The following app visualizes the functioning of the Mohr Circle. It explains how the Mohr Circle can be used to
identify the different stresses that act on a differental element with variable orientation.

In this application, the following interactivities have been modelled:

The normal forces in x- and in z-direction can be changed , similary the tangential force can be changed.
This app also gives an idea about stress values if the cross section is at any other angle than the original one.
