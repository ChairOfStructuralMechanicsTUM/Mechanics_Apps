Let us now consider the equilibrium of forces in the horizontal and vertical directions in the free body diagram of a cable car, $$ \sum F_x:\quad T_2 \: cos \: \theta_2 - T_1 \: cos \: \theta_1 =0. $$ $$ \sum F_y:\quad T_2 \: sin \: \theta_2 - T_1 \: sin \: \theta_1 - W =0. $$

After configuring the parameters of cable car system and the load, the user can interactively visualize the equilibrium position of the car and compute the cable tensions.

Hint: The cable length stretch factor c is always larger than 1. However it can be very close to 1, such that the slider shows the value 1 whereas the actual value is 1.001.
