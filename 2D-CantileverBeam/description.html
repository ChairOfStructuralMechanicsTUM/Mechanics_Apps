Cantilever Beam

In the following app, The 3D cantilever beam has been modelled in two seperate 2D plots. The deformation the beams below are going to show is based on the solution of Euler-Bernouli Ordinary Differential Equation. The colors that will be seen in the plots correspond to the distribution of normal stresses.
