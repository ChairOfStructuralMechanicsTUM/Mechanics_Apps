Moment_2

The following app gives introduction to Moment Vector .It explains how different forces can produce same Moment vector.

In this application, the following interactivities have been modelled:

--- The Forces P1 and P2 together makes a Moment of 400Nm at the pivot.The position of forces F1&F2 can be changed using the slider on right . Moment created by F1&F2 is made to balanc that made by P1 & P2 . Corresponding change in magnitude of Forces are plotted and visualised
