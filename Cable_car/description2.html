The following trigonometric relations can be obtained by considering the kinematic configuration of the cable car: $$L_x \: cos \: \theta_1 = x,$$ $$(L-L_x) \: cos \: \theta_2 = B-x,$$ $$L_x \: sin\: \theta_1 + (L-L_x) \: sin\: \theta_2 = H$$ where $L_x$ is the bottom cable segment length, $B$ is the horizontal distance between the supports and $H$ is the vertical distance between the supports. The angles of the cable at the supports ($\theta_1$ and $\theta_2$) can be found by simultaneously solving these trigonometric relations.
