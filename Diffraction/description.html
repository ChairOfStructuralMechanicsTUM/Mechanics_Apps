Diffraction of sound waves at noise barriers

In the following app we visualize the diffraction of planar waves at a cutting edge. This phenomena is important in the context of sound barriers at traffic routes: Sound power is transported to the shadow area of the barrier by diffraction.

We allow the modification of wavelength λ and angle of incidence φ0 of the waves. By clicking inside the countour plot, the noise level at this spot in dB can be probed and, therefore, the noise level at different positions and the effectivity of the barrier can be quantified.
