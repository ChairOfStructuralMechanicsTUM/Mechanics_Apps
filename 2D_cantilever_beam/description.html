Cantilever Beam

This application models the deformation and normal stresses of a 3D cantilever beam based on the solution of the Euler-Bernoulli Ordinary Differential Equation. The deflection is calculated performing a fourfold integral of the load

The beam is subjected to a force that can be separated into a vertical component Fy and into a horizontal component Fz. The value of this load can be changed using the two sliders on the right. The results are then shown in two separate 2D plots for the X-Y and X-Z plane, where the colors of the beam correspond to the distribution of the normal stresses.
