Cantilever Beam

This application models the deformation and normal stresses of a 3D cantilever beam based on the solution of the Euler-Bernoulli Ordinary Differential Equation.

The beam is subjected to force that can be separated into a vertical component Fy and to a horizontal component Fz. The values of this load can be changed using the two sliders to the right. The results are then shown in two separate 2D plots for the X-Y and X-Z plane, where the colors of the beam correspond to the distribution of the normal stresses.
