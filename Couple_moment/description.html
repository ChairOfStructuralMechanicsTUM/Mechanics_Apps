The couple-moment

The following app gives an introduction to the concept of moment generated by a couple. A couple is formed by any two equal parallel forces that have opposite senses. It explains how different couples can produce the same moment vector.

The Forces \(P_1\) and \(P_2\) together makes a Moment of 400 Nm at the pivot.The position of forces \(F_1\) and \(F_2\) can be changed using the slider on the right . By decreasing the distance between the couple \(F_1\) and \(F_2\), the amplitude of the force needs to be increased in order to balanc the couple-moment of the couple \(P_1\) and \(P_2\). Corresponding change in magnitude of Forces are visualised.
