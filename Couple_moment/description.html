Moment generated by pairs of forces

The following app gives an introduction to the concept of a moment generated by a pair of forces. This pair is formed by two parallel forces with the same value but opposite direction. This app shows how different pairs can produce the same moment vector.

The forces $P$ generate a moment of $400\,$Nm at the pivot. The position $b$ of the force pair $F$ can be changed using the slider on the right. By decreasing the distance between the pair $F$, the amplitude of the forces needs to be increased in order to balance the moment generated by the pair $P$: \[ \curvearrowright\sum M = 2Pa - 2Fb = 0. \] The corresponding change in the magnitude of the forces is visualised.
