The couple-moment

The following app gives an introduction to the concept of moment generated by a couple. A couple is formed by any two equal parallel forces with opposite senses. This app shows how different couples can produce the same moment vector.

The forces $P_1$ and $P_2$ together generate a moment of 400 Nm at the pivot. The position of the forces $F_1$ and $F_2$ can be changed using the slider on the right. By decreasing the distance between the couple $F_1$ and $F_2$, the amplitude of the force needs to be increased in order to balance the couple-moment of the couple $P_1$ and $P_2$. The corresponding change in the magnitude of the forces is visualised.
