The couple-moment

The following app gives an introduction to the concept of moment generated by a couple. A couple is formed by any two equal parallel forces with opposite senses. This app shows how different couples can produce the same moment vector.

The forces P1 and P2 together make a moment of 400 Nm at the pivot. The position of the forces F1 and F2 can be changed using the slider on the right. By decreasing the distance between the couple F1 and F2, the amplitude of the force needs to be increased in order to balance the couple-moment of the couple P1 and P2. The corresponding change in the magnitude of the forces is visualised.
