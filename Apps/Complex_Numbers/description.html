COMPLEX NUMBERS

This app visualizes complex numbers and their arithmetic in the complex plane. A complex number $Z = a + ib$ can either be defined via real part $a$ and imaginary part $b$ or by choosing the absolute value $| Z | = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2}$ and the phase angle $\alpha$; with $tan(\alpha) = \frac{Im(Z)}{Re(Z)}$.

In this app the user can choose two complex numbers $Z1$ and $Z2$ by defining their absolute value $| Z | $ and phase angle $\alpha$ using the sliders below. Pushing the green button $Z2$ will be set to the complex conjugate of $Z1$.

Besides $Z1$ and $Z2$, their addition $Z3$, subtraction $Z4$, multiplication $Z5$ and division $Z6$ are plotted in the complex plane. Real and imaginary part of the complex numbers can be extracted from the plot and the table on the right.
