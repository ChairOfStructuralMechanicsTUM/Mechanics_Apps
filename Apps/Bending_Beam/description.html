Beam in Bending

The app visualizes the internal forces of a beam in bending under a user defined loading. The beam of length L is supported by a fixed support on the left and by a movable support with adjustable position on the right. If the user sets the position of the movable support to zero, the beam is clamped.
Beneath the system, the shear force and the bending moment over the lenght of the beam is plotted.

The interactivity parameters are

  • Load type (point load, line load, triangular load)
  • Load position/length
  • Load amplitude
  • Position of movable support
  • Revealing/Hiding support forces
