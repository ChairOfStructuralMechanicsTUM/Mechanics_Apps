Tensile test

This app visualizes the tensile test of three different materials. Tensile testing is a destructive test process, where a sample is subjected to a controlled, unidirectional force until failure. This test provides information about various properties of the tested material.

The user can choose from three different materials:

  • Steel: Ductile material with a pronounced yield strength. After reaching the tensile strength the sample begins to neck and the cross-section is reduced.
  • Rubber: Very elastic material that can undergo high deformations. When the sample is loaded and unloaded the stress-strain curve describes a hysteresis loop.
  • Carbon fiber reinforced polymer (CFRP): Brittle material that breaks with little elastic deformation and without significant plastic deformation. The yield strength is not pronounced.
