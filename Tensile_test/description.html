Tensile test

This app visualizes the tensile test of three different materials. Tensile testing is a destructive test process, where a sample is subjected to a controlled, unidirectional force until failure. This test provides information about the tensile strength, the yield strength and the ductility of the tested material.

The user can choose from three different materials:

  • Steel
  • Rubber
  • Carbon fiber reinforced polymer (CFRP)

The deformation of the sample is shown in the left figure and the stress-strain diagram is plotted on the right.
