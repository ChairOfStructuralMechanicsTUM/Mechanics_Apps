Damped oscillator

This app shows the motion of a damped oscillator, made up of a mass hanged from the end of a spring-dashpot element subjected to self weight and to a given initial velocity. By starting the oscillation, the system oscillates about the static equilibrium position.

The interactivity parameters are

  • the initial velocity of the mass in m/s
  • the mass in kg
  • the spring stiffness in N/m
  • the damping constant in Ns/m

For high values of the damping, the oscillation amplitude decays quickly and the system reaches its static equilibrium. Zero initial velocity means that the displacement starts with a horizontal tangent.

At the right-top corner of the diagram, several tools allow to zoom in or to scroll forward in time. The reset button brings the system back to the initial settings.
