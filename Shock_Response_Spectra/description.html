Shock response spectra of Damped SDOF system

This app shows the motion of a classical single degree of freedom system, cosisting out of a discrete mass, a spring, and a damper element. due to a shock force of duration of (0,t_0). This shock force can be selected from the dropdown list consist of rectangular, triangular or sinusoidal force. The maximum value of shock force is always choosen to be 1 Newton. By starting the oscillation, the system oscillates around the static equilibrium position at $u = 0$. The displacement plot is normalized with the spring stiffness. No gravity is acting on the system.

The interactivity parameters are

  • the damping constant in Ns/m
  • Impulse duration to natural period
