Instant center of rotation

For very small displacements the movement of a rigid body can be described as pure rotation around an instant centre of rotation (ICR). Thereby the ICR is not deflected.

The following app shows the ICR of a ladder which is leaned to a wall. The inclination of the ladder with respect to the vertical wall can be changed by the user, and the new ICR will be plotted. Furthermore, the magnitudes of vertical and horizontal displacements of the ladder ends corresponding to the current centre of rotation are visualized.
