COMPLEX NUMBERS

This app visualizes complex numbers in the complex plane. A complex number $Z = a + ib$ can be defined via real part $a$ and imaginary part $b$. An other option to define $Z$ is choosing the absolute value $| Z | = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2}$ and the phase angle $\alpha$ using $tan(\alpha) = \frac{Im(Z)}{Re(Z)}$

In this app the user can choose two complex numbers $Z1$ and $Z2$ by defining their absolute value and phase angle using the sliders below. Real and imaginary part of $Z1$ and $Z2$ can be extracted from the plot. Pushing the green button $Z2$ will be set to the complex conjugate of $Z1$.

Besides $Z1$ and $Z2$, their addition $Z3$, subtraction $Z4$, Multiplication $Z5$ and Division $Z6$ are plotted in the complex plane.
