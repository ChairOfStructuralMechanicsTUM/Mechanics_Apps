The first picture displays the beam and its supports as well as the position of the applied load.
The type of load can be selected by clicking on one of the buttons on the left. Use the radio buttons to change the support types, and the slider to move the load position.
The second and third picture shows the resulting normal force and deformation respectively. Using the tools on the right one can zoom in and out along the y-axis to have a better look at the graphs.
Since every zero crossing of the normal force graph corresponds to an extreme value of the deformation at this point, you can click on "Show line" to check this observation.
Use the reset button to restore the standard settings.
