Normal Force Rod

The app visualizes the normal force $N(x)$ and the horizontal displacement $u(x)$ of a rod under a user defined loading and support configuration.
Beneath the system, the normal force and the horizontal displacement over the lenght $L$ of the rod is plotted.

The calculations are based on the following differential equation: $u^{''}(x) = \left ( \frac{N(x)}{EA} \right)^{'} + \left( \alpha_T T(x) \right )^{'}$;
the Young's modulus $E$, the cross section $A$ and the temperature coefficient $\alpha_T$ are assumed to be constant along the length of the rod.

The interactivity parameters are

  • Load type (point load, line load, triangular load, temperature load)
  • Load position/length
  • Load amplitude
  • Support configuration
If the rod is supported by two fixed supports the, user can click on "Show line" to check on the magnitude $u_{max}(x_a)$ and position $x_a$ of the maximum horizontal displacement along the rod.
